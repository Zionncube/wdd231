About MindWink Academy

Empowering students to unlock their divine potential.

Our Mission

To help students harmonize their spiritual, mental, and physical selves in their academic journey by offering practical strategies grounded in faith and science.

Our Vision

We envision a generation of confident, purpose-driven learners who achieve greatness with God at the center of their success.

Meet the Founder

Happiness Ncube, a passionate youth mentor and web developer-in-training, founded MindWink Academy to share life-changing insights rooted in experience, education, and faith. Her mission is to uplift others with tools that build confidence, discipline, and self-awareness.
